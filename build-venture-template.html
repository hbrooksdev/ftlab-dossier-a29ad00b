FTLAB Venture-Org Template — v1.0 (fractal)

Status: Internal / Strategic / Confidential · 2026-06-02 · Phase 3 Supersedes: ../../drafts/ftlab-venture-org-template-v0.1.md (the seven-Domain version). Reframed to the determined Core + ~3 Engines + Edge.

Every venture FTLAB builds instantiates the same shape at its own scale — that is how governance scales without a management chain (a self-similar pattern, not a lengthening hierarchy). This is the second tier of the fractal; the first is the studio (operating-manual.md).

1. The venture is a Core · Engines · Edge instance

LayerAt the studioAt a venture
Edgethe founders (Architect/Intent-Setter/Guardian)the venture's own small human cortex (a founder/operator + studio-appointed Guardian), holding the same three functions
EnginesVenture · Platform · Knowledge & Ecosystemthe venture's own value-creating functions, sized to its business — typically Product/Delivery, Growth/GTM, and Operations; not a copy of the studio's three
Corethe studio's Machine Coreinherited — the venture runs on the studio's Core/Platform; it does not build its own

2. Inherited vs. the venture's own

  • Inherited from the studio (the genome + the substrate): the frozen lexicon, the Values-as-code (hardConstraints), the Agent Charter schema, the Machine Core (memory, integrator, IPRE/PRAL, the Governance Loop, the oversight scripts), and the licensed Platform. A venture starts governed, not greenfield.
  • The venture's own: its Edge (cortex), its Engine charters (sized to its work and sector), its Strategy-as-Code (its own intent/preferences), its decision trace.

3. The venture's own Guardian and independence

The venture has its own Guardian (in its cortex), with studio oversight: the venture Guardian holds the local halt-right; the studio Guardian-auditor reviews the venture's override-rate and Alignment-Debt dashboards and can compel a halt. The same hard rule applies — the venture Guardian carries no venture P&L. At a tiny venture this is met as at the studio: the cortex member without the P&L stake guards each decision, and the external (studio) halt-right is the independence anchor brought forward.

4. Sector-dependent sizing (no fixed ratio)

Each Engine's agent population and default oversight are set by risk, reversibility, and regulatory intensity:

  • Regulated / trust-bearing ventures (e.g. finance, clinical): default to tighter oversight — more R3/R4 actions at human-escalation, lower autonomy thresholds, more evaluator coverage. Adaptic (finance) is the worked case — see go-live-adaptic.md.
  • Low-stakes, reversible ventures: larger, more autonomous Engines; autonomy widens faster.

5. Growth across the pipeline

A venture moves Thesis → Prototype → Named → Growth (the Venture Engine's life-stage sub-charters). At each stage its Engines' autonomy widens by measured reliability, never by date, and symmetrically (Core autonomy widens ↔ the matching human/face mandate withdraws; failure-to-thin = Alignment Debt). Capital and irreversible actions escalate to the venture Edge — and, above a threshold, to the studio Edge — at every stage.

6. How the studio governs without a chain

  • Single reporting line: the venture reports to the studio Edge only (never dual-reported to a function head) — dual reporting degrades autonomy and is forbidden (Book 1 Ch 10).
  • The studio governs the genome, not daily execution: it sets the inherited Values and the Core; it does not run the venture's work. Genuine venture autonomy is a condition of the Mothership model, not a courtesy.
  • Cross-venture learning flows through the shared Core (shared agent library, federated learning) — the Knowledge Flywheel. What one venture learns, the next inherits.
  • Honest exit criteria are pre-written per venture (Book 1 Ch 10): if the model itself is failing (e.g., three consecutive ventures miss profitability in 24 months), review the model, not just execution.

7. The permanent floor

A venture can devolve doing and coordinating to its agents, but never accountability, values authorship, or the final halt — those stay with its cortex (and the studio's), by legitimacy and law, not by capability. Maximal autonomy is the smallest human layer that can hold those, not zero humans.
